How This Works
Thank you for your interest. Some items to keep in mind:.
The sample videos on the main page show images and text which can be substituted for your images and text.
When you find a video that you want to use, click on "buy now," and after paying, email whatever text and images go with the video to verba001 @ gmail.com.
If you don't have images, we can provide images for an extra fee ($3).
It is possible to combine videos. (Please indicate this when or after ordering.)
The default format is the .mp4 format. If you wish a .gif format, please state this when ordering.
If your details don't arrive within 72 hours, the order will be canceled and the price will be refunded.
If you aren't happy with the video, you can ask for one set of alterations. If you're still not happy, please email within 72 hours and you will be refunded. No refunds will be issued after 72 hours of purchase.
